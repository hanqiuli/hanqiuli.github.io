# On Writing and Thinking

Writing is thinking made visible. When you write, you're forced to organize your thoughts, find the gaps, and fill them.

## The Process

I don't outline much. I start with a question or an observation, then let it unfold. Sometimes a post takes 20 minutes. Sometimes it takes weeks of sitting with an idea.

## Tools

I write in plain text. Markdown, specifically. No fancy apps, no distraction-free modes. Just a text editor and my thoughts.

The simpler the tool, the more I focus on the writing itself.

## Advice

If you want to write more, the secret is simple: write more. Don't wait for inspiration. Don't wait for the perfect topic. Just write something, anything, and publish it.

The best time to start a blog was ten years ago. The second best time is today.
